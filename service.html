Our Services

Solar Panels

Solar Resource Analysis & Feasibility Studies We conduct in-depth evaluations of solar resources to identify the most suitable sites for solar projects. This involves analyzing sunlight exposure and variability throughout the year. Our process includes drone-assisted topographical surveys to gather precise data on land features and conditions. By assessing these factors, we ensure that the site will optimize solar energy capture and meet all project feasibility requirements. Our goal is to provide accurate recommendations that maximize the efficiency and viability of solar installations.

Wind Turbines

Wind Micro-Siting & Energy Yield Analysis We carry out detailed assessments of wind conditions at various heights to determine the best locations for wind turbines. This includes measuring wind speed, direction, and variability through advanced technology and data analysis. Our energy yield analyses use this data to predict how much power each turbine will generate under different conditions. This helps in optimizing turbine placement for maximum efficiency and performance. Our comprehensive approach ensures that wind projects are set up to achieve their full potential.

Land Acquisition

We manage the technical and logistical aspects of connecting renewable energy projects to the existing power grid. This involves coordinating with utility companies and regulatory bodies to meet all connectivity requirements. Our team handles the acquisition of land, whether through purchase or lease, to ensure that the project has the necessary space and legal agreements in place. We also address any potential obstacles that could delay project development. Our focus is on streamlining the process to facilitate smooth and efficient project implementation.

Design & Construction

We specialize in the design and construction of pooling sub-stations that are crucial for integrating power from multiple sources. These sub-stations, with capacities of 33/220 or 400KV, play a key role in managing and distributing generated electricity across the grid. Our engineering solutions ensure that these facilities are built to handle the power load efficiently and reliably. We oversee every phase of construction to maintain high standards of safety and performance. Our aim is to deliver robust infrastructure that supports the stability and efficiency of power distribution.

Power Transmission

We provide comprehensive services for designing and constructing power transmission systems that transport electricity from generation sites to the grid. This includes developing evacuation systems to facilitate the smooth flow of power. We handle the necessary grid connections and address any right-of-way (ROW) issues that may arise. Our expertise ensures that the transmission infrastructure is capable of handling the energy load and operating reliably. We focus on creating efficient systems that support the successful delivery of power to end-users.

PMC Services

We develop essential infrastructure components such as fencing, access roads, lighting, and weather stations to support renewable energy projects. Our services include Project Management Consultancy (PMC) to oversee the execution of these projects and ensure they stay on schedule. We also handle all necessary permits and approvals required by regulatory authorities. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive suite of services that address both operational and regulatory needs. By managing these elements effectively, we help ensure the successful completion and operation of energy projects.
